## Standout facts worth featuring

- **Nora floors absorb up to 20 dB of footfall noise** — a measurable acoustic improvement that hospitals can quote in their noise-reduction reports.
- **Nora is on every Canadian health-authority master spec** — Ottawa Hospital, CHEO, Royal, Montfort, Bruyère, Queensway Carleton all spec Nora by name.
- **Nora has been part of Interface since 2018** — that means YCI's Interface relationship covers BOTH carpet tile AND healthcare rubber. Cross-product credibility.
- **PVC-free / phthalate-free** — Nora and Mondo both meet Living Building Challenge Red List avoidance, which puts them on every modern healthcare LEED v4.1 spec.
- **Rubber doesn't accept wax** — the maintenance cycle is simpler than VCT and cheaper to operate over the floor's 20+ year lifespan.
