## Standout facts worth featuring

- **80%+ of building dirt enters via foot traffic** — the matting industry's foundational stat.
- **15 ft of entrance matting captures ~85% of soil** — 25 ft captures **>95%**. ISSA research.
- **Ottawa's 6-month winter salt/slush/grit cycle** makes undersized matting a measurable liability — salt damage to LVT, hardwood, and carpet is the #2 callback YCI sees on commercial entrances.
- **Construction Specialties Pedimat** is the spec name dropped in every Class A federal building in Ottawa — anchored aluminium rails with replaceable inserts.
- **Forbo Coral tufted face yarn captures 85% of dirt within 3–4 footsteps** — the surface walk-off complement to recessed grid.
