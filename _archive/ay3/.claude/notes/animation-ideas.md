## Matting

**[SELECTED] Foot-Traffic Heatmap**
Bird's-eye lobby entrance. Heatmap pulses show traffic density (hot near doors, cooling inward). Matting zone overlay fades in to show how zone sizing maps to traffic patterns. Teaches *how much* matting to spec.

**Backups:**
- **Cross-Section Anatomy** — Side cutaway builds up: recessed grille below grade → matting drops in → surrounding floor flush. Labels each layer.
- **Zone Performance Gradient** — Top-down entrance, shoe-print trail across 3 zones (scrape/wipe/dry), dirt opacity fades per step.
- **Depth Comparison** — Side-by-side: proper recessed (flush) vs surface-laid (trip hazard lip). Red highlight on the bad one.
- **Seasonal Swap** — Matting insert lifts out of recessed well, different insert drops in. Communicates replaceable system.

## Carpet (Broadloom)

**[SELECTED] Noise Path — Above & Below**
Two-story cutaway. Upstairs walk → sound waves penetrate ceiling downstairs. Carpet + pad installs → same footstep, waves caught, downstairs quiet. Before/after toggle.

**Backups:**
- **Sound Absorption Cross-Section** — Hard floor vs carpet+pad side-by-side, sound waves bounce vs absorb. IIC/STC labels.
- **Impact Comfort Underfoot** — Foot steps on hard vs carpet, pressure map heatmap shows stress distribution difference.
- **Thermal Insulation Layer Cake** — Concrete (cold/blue) → pad → carpet (warm/amber), R-value label. Temperature gradient.
- **Drop Test / Safety** — Object falls on hard floor (shatters) vs carpet (bounces). Senior living / childcare safety angle.

## LVT / VCT / SCT

**[SELECTED] Layer Exploded View**
Three columns. Each product explodes vertically to reveal internal layers. LVT: wear→print→core→backing. VCT: single homogeneous body. SCT: wear→stone core→backing. Shows sandwich vs solid vs rigid.

**Backups:**
- **Flex / Rigidity Test** — Force pushes down on each over subfloor imperfection. LVT bends, VCT telegraphs, SCT bridges flat.
- **Wear & Refinish Cycle** — VCT dulls then strip-&-wax restores; LVT/SCT wear layer thins, can't refinish. Maintenance tradeoff.
- **Click vs Glue-Down Install** — Split view: plank clicks in (floating) vs tile lays into adhesive (glue-down).
- **Subfloor Moisture Path** — Concrete slab moisture rises. VCT: adhesion failure. SCT rigid core: blocked. Slab-on-grade spec argument.

## Rubber

**[SELECTED] Impact / Drop Absorption by Thickness**
Dumbbell drops onto 3 rubber thicknesses (6mm/8mm/12mm). Thin: impact reaches subfloor (red). Thick: fully absorbed (green). Shows why gyms need thicker rubber.
**+ Text note:** Recycled tire origin story (shredded crumb → compressed → finished tile, sustainability angle).

**Backups:**
- **Recycled Tire Journey** — 3-step: shredded tire → compressed/bound → finished speckle tile. *(Moved to text, but could be animated.)*
- **Vulcanized vs Recycled Cross-Section** — Dense uniform (vulcanized, hospitals) vs visible aggregate (recycled, gyms).
- **Chemical / Stain Resistance Wipe** — Drops land (bleach, betadine, hydraulic fluid), wipe cleans rubber vs stains on carpet/VCT.
- **Sound & Vibration Isolation** — Fitness studio above occupied space, equipment vibration dampened by rubber before reaching slab.
